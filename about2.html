About us

Anime Journal is a shopping website dedicated for selling Anime-related merchandises. We offer different types of Anime merchandise, such as mangas, figurines, mousepads, and many more. Our site is approved by the government law, and all transactions are legal and security of products and personal information are assured. The site offers refund, if the issues come from our side, due to delivery delay, damage during delivery, and other issues relating to the process of transaction that results from using our site. We have trustworthy sellers from all around the globe, ranging not only from Japan and US, there are also from China, Malaysia, Australia, and other countries. Anime Journal is the finest option for purchasing anime merchandise; we are professional that enjoy giving all of you anime lovers the greatest experience possible.
